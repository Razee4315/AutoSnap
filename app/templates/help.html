Help & About

Quick Start Guide

  1. Click "Start Capturing" on the home page
  2. Configure your capture settings
  3. Choose a save location for your screenshots
  4. Click "Start Capturing" to begin

  • Capture Frequency: Set how often screenshots are taken
  • Auto Detection: Automatically detect and capture board content
  • Image Quality: Adjust the quality of saved screenshots

  • View all screenshots in the Screenshot Manager
  • Search and filter screenshots by date
  • Download or delete selected screenshots

Frequently Asked Questions

AutoSnap uses advanced computer vision algorithms to detect changes in board content and automatically captures screenshots when significant changes are detected.

Screenshots are saved to the location you specify in Capture Settings. By default, they are saved in your Documents folder under "AutoSnap/Screenshots".

About AutoSnap

AutoSnap is an intelligent software tool designed for recording video lectures and automatically capturing important content displayed on boards.
